About Me

Hey there! I'm Remofilwe Pearl Semaushu, but you can call me Remo Pearl. Music has always been a big part of my life, but it all started with a simple moment that I'll never forget. One day, when I was just 9 years old, my mom asked me to write down the lyrics to one of her favorite songs, Ronan Keating's "If Tomorrow Never Comes". So there I was, carefully writing out each word, and before I knew it, I fell in love with lyrics and music.

It became a hobby of mine—writing the lyrics to all my favorite songs (haha). I even still have that last lyric file tucked away somewhere. Over time, I thought, "Hey, maybe I can write my own song!" And that's how the passion and love for creating music was born, all thanks to my mom and her favorite song. So, Mom, if you're reading this, thank you! You planted the seed that grew into something incredible. XO

About Worth It

Worth It features seven tracks about letting go, having fun, and embracing self-love. It captures the freedom of shedding past worries and stepping into a space where joy and confidence reign. The music radiates a playful energy, encouraging listeners to let loose, live in the moment, and celebrate life without holding back.

At its core, Worth It champions the power of self-acceptance. It’s a reminder to love yourself fully, with all your flaws, and to recognize your worth. Through each beat and melody, the message is clear: let go of what doesn't serve you, have fun unapologetically, and always choose to love yourself first.
